Inside the dark

Inside the dark is a dungeon-crawler game about exploring the dark dungeon. I did this project with a team of 3 people (Artem Urazovsky, Sergey Belmas, and Alexey Grabar), in which I was the leader and programmer. In the beginning, we developed this game specifically for the ITalent competition. We made the first version in literally two months and took the 5th absolute place nationally. We are still developing the game, but you can already watch the trailer and listen to some soundtrack from our composer Artem Urazovsky.

Scientific Research

The topic of my research is "Simulation of light propagation in an optically inhomogeneous medium". Although this project is in the "Theoretical Physics" section, a considerable part of it is the description of the ray path construction algorithm. For implementation, I used the C# programming language together with XNA and Unity engines.
